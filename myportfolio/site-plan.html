Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to create a portfolio website that showcases my skills, experience, and projects in web development and design. It will include sections such as a professional bio, résumé, completed project samples, and contact information.

Audience

The intended audience is prospective employers, recruiters, and potential clients who may be interested in hiring me for internships,freelance work, or full-time positions in web development or design.

Content

Home page

About Me Hi! I'm Rocio Orellana, an web designer currently studying at BYU–Idaho. I'm passionate about building user-friendly websites and creating visually compelling digital experiences. This personal portfolio is a snapshot of who I am, what I create, and where I’m headed in the world of web development. My Mission I believe that great design is about solving problems while looking good. My mission is to combine clean, intuitive design with strong functionality to create websites that not only work well but also leave a lasting impression. What This Website Offers This site is more than just a collection of my work—it's a place to learn about me, explore my process, and see the skills I've developed so far. Whether you’re a potential employer, client, or peer, you’ll find examples of what I can do and how to get in touch.

Projects Page - My work

Web Development Projects Here you’ll find a variety of projects I’ve worked on during my time at BYU–Idaho and beyond. From simple HTML/CSS landing pages to more advanced JavaScript and React applications, each project has taught me something valuable. I include short descriptions of the project goals, tools used, and the challenges I overcame. Design Portfolio In addition to coding, I’ve worked on the design side of web development. I enjoy using tools like Figma and Adobe XD to plan UI/UX layouts before coding begins. You’ll see design mockups, wireframes, and branding examples here that show my attention to visual hierarchy, color schemes, and typography. Technical Skills Each project includes the technologies I used—like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Git, and more. I also highlight any group collaboration or Agile practices I followed to bring the project to life. These skills are constantly growing as I learn new frameworks and design trends.

Contact Page

Why Reach Out? If you’re interested in working together, have questions about my work, or just want to connect, I’d love to hear from you. I'm currently open to internships, freelance opportunities, and collaborations on creative projects. How to Get in Touch You can fill out the simple contact form on this page, and I’ll get back to you as soon as possible. I’m also happy to provide more details about any of the projects listed or send a copy of my resume. Available and Responsive I try to respond within 24–48 hours and enjoy meaningful conversations about design, technology, or career opportunities. Feel free to ask questions—even if you’re just curious about how I built something on my site!
